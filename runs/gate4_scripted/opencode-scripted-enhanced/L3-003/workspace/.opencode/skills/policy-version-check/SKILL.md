# Policy Version Check

Use policy tools to compare the business period with publication, effective, expiry, repeal, replacement, and transition dates. Cite only retrieved documents and clauses. Explain why the selected policy applies and why competing versions do not.

For an unambiguous current policy, use a compact direct lookup. Use a policy researcher only for genuine multi-version conflict or unresolved applicability; the script route does not make delegation mandatory.
