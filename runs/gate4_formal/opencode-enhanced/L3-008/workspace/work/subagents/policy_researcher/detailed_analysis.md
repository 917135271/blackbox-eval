| 城市档位 | 培训住宿标准 |
|----------|:------------:|
| 一类城市 | 500 |

Key difference: Training standards are uniform for all employees regardless of grade. Travel standards vary by grade.

## 4. Explicit Non-Mixing Clauses

### 4a. 04_travel_expense.md — Article 7 (Chapter 5, 附则)
> 第七条 培训期间住宿不适用本办法住宿表，应按《XX证券培训费管理办法》执行。

This clause explicitly removes training-period accommodation from the travel accommodation table's scope and redirects to the training expense policy.

### 4b. 05_training_expense.md — Article 6 (Chapter 3, 管理要求)
> 第六条 培训住宿标准仅适用于培训期间住宿，不得与差旅住宿标准混用。

This clause restricts the training accommodation standard to training periods only and expressly forbids mixing with travel accommodation standards.

### 4c. 01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md — Article 12 (Chapter 4, 禁止性规定)
> 第十二条 无专项审批时，报销金额不得超过对应制度标准的1.0倍。

This overarching clause applies to all expense types and reinforces that whichever standard applies (travel or training), the reimbursement cannot exceed that standard without special approval.

## 6. Why Mixing Is Prohibited

The two standards cannot be mixed for the following reasons:

1. **Different regulatory basis**: Travel accommodation is governed by 04_travel_expense.md; training accommodation is governed by 05_training_expense.md. These are independent policy documents with distinct 发文字号.

2. **Different standard dimensions**: Travel accommodation uses a 4×3 matrix (grade × city tier), while training accommodation uses a single-dimensional table (city tier only). Training standards are intentionally uniform across all employee grades.

3. **Explicit mutual exclusion**:
   - 04_travel_expense.md Article 7: Training-period accommodation is explicitly excluded from the travel standard table.
   - 05_training_expense.md Article 6: Training accommodation standards must not be mixed with travel standards.

4. **Overarching compliance**: 01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md Article 12 prohibits exceeding the applicable policy standard without special approval, meaning substitution of a higher standard from the wrong policy would constitute a violation.

5. **Practical effect**: An employee-level employee on training in a Tier-1 city would be entitled to 500元 under the training policy but only 450元 under the travel policy — a difference of 50元. Using the wrong policy could result in either underpayment or overpayment, both of which represent non-compliance.

---

## 7. Conclusion

The two policies are completely separate, with distinct standard tables, explicit mutual exclusion clauses, and an overarching prohibition against exceeding applicable standards without special approval. Any attempt to apply a travel accommodation standard to training accommodation (or vice versa) would violate:
- 04_travel_expense.md Article 7
- 05_training_expense.md Article 6
- 01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md Article 12

The comparison is conclusive; no unresolved items remain.
