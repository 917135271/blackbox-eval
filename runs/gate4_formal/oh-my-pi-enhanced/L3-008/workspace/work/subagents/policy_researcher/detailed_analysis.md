## 2. Which Policy Governs What

### 2a. Travel Lodging (差旅住宿)

**Governing policy**: `04_travel_expense.md` — XX证券差旅费管理办法

- **Article 2 (第二条)**: Defines travel expenses to include lodging (住宿费) as part of business travel.
- **Article 3 (第三条)**: Establishes that lodging standards are determined by **employee level** (员工级, 经理级, 部门负责人级, 高管级) crossed with **city tier** (一类城市, 二类城市, 三类城市).
- **Article 4 (第四条)**: Provides the exact numerical lodging limits.
- **Article 7 (第七条)**: Explicitly **excludes** training-period lodging from this policy's lodging table. Cross-references the training expense policy.

### 2b. Training Lodging (培训住宿)

**Governing policy**: `05_training_expense.md` — XX证券培训费管理办法

- **Article 5 (第五条)**: Provides training lodging standards by city tier only (no employee level dimension).
- **Article 6 (第六条)**: Explicitly prohibits mixing training lodging standards with travel lodging standards.

### 2c. Cross-References in the General Reimbursement Policy

`01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md` — XX证券费用报销管理办法(2025修订版):

- **Article 2 (第二条)**: Confirms the general policy applies to both 差旅 (travel) and 培训 (training) expense types.
- **Article 12 (第十二条)**: Caps reimbursement at 1.0× the applicable policy standard absent special approval — this ceiling applies to both travel and training lodging.

## 3. Standard Dimensions

### 3a. Travel Lodging Dimensions (04_travel_expense.md, Article 3)

A **two-dimensional matrix**:

- **Dimension 1 — Employee Level** (职级): 4 levels
  1. 员工级 (Staff)
  2. 经理级 (Manager)
  3. 部门负责人级 (Department Head)
  4. 高管级 (Senior Executive)

- **Dimension 2 — City Tier** (城市档位): 3 tiers
  1. 一类城市 (Tier 1)
  2. 二类城市 (Tier 2)
  3. 三类城市 (Tier 3)

**Total**: 4 × 3 = 12 distinct standards.

### 3b. Training Lodging Dimensions (05_training_expense.md, Article 5)

A **single dimension**:

- **Dimension — City Tier** (城市档位): 3 tiers only
  1. 一类城市 (Tier 1)
  2. 二类城市 (Tier 2)
  3. 三类城市 (Tier 3)

**No employee level differentiation**. Single flat rate per city tier regardless of the trainee's seniority.

**Total**: 3 distinct standards.

## 7. Why the Two Policies Cannot Be Mixed

### 7a. Different Scope (适用范围不同)

- **Travel policy (04)**: Applies to 公务出差 (official business travel). The triggering event is a business trip — meetings, client visits, inter-office travel, etc.
- **Training policy (05)**: Applies to 培训活动 (training activities), both internal (内部培训) and external (外部培训). The triggering event is participation in a training program.

These are mutually exclusive triggering events. An employee is either traveling on business or attending training — never both for the same lodging night.

### 7b. Different Standard Dimensions (标准维度不同)

- **Travel lodging**: 4×3 matrix (employee level × city tier) → 12 distinct standards. Seniority matters: a department head gets a higher lodging allowance than a staff member.
- **Training lodging**: 1×3 vector (city tier only) → 3 distinct standards. Seniority is irrelevant: all trainees share the same allowance.

The training policy deliberately flattens the hierarchy — the rationale is that training is a collective activity where uniform accommodation is expected.

### 7c. Different Numerical Values (数值不同)

As demonstrated in Section 6, the training standards (500/420/350) do not correspond to any single employee level in the travel table. In all three city tiers, the training standard falls between the 员工级 and 经理级 travel standards. There is no principled way to "convert" between them.

### 7d. Explicit Mutual Exclusion Clauses (明示互斥条款)

Both policies contain explicit non-mixing language:

- **04_travel_expense.md, Article 7**: "培训期间住宿不适用本办法住宿表,应按《XX证券培训费管理办法》执行。"
  _(Training-period lodging does not apply to this policy's lodging table; follow the Training Expense Management Method.)_

- **05_training_expense.md, Article 6**: "培训住宿标准仅适用于培训期间住宿,不得与差旅住宿标准混用。"
  _(Training lodging standards apply only to training-period lodging; must not be mixed with travel lodging standards.)_

These are not recommendations — they are prohibitions (`不得` = MUST NOT).

### 7e. General Policy Ceiling (通用政策上限)

- **01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md, Article 12**: "无专项审批时,报销金额不得超过对应制度标准的1.0倍。"
  _(Without special approval, reimbursement must not exceed 1.0× the applicable policy standard.)_

This applies uniformly but does not resolve which standard is "applicable" — that determination is made by the scope-triggering event (business travel vs. training), not by which standard is more favorable to the employee.

---

## 8. Conclusion

The two lodging regimes are **independent and non-overlapping**. They are triggered by different activities (business travel vs. training), use different dimensions (employee level + city tier vs. city tier only), yield different numerical values at every city tier, and are explicitly prohibited from cross-application by clauses in both policies. An auditor determining whether a lodging expense complies with policy must first establish whether the expense arose from business travel or training, then apply the corresponding policy standard.
