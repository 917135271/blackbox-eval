## 2. Replacement / Repeal Relationship

The 2025 revision **explicitly repeals and replaces** the 2022 version.

**Direct repeal clause** in `01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md`, Article 14 (第十四条):
> "原《XX证券费用报销管理办法(2022版)》同时废止。"
> (The original XX Securities Expense Reimbursement Management Measures (2022 Edition) is simultaneously abolished.)

The 2022 version **acknowledges its own deprecation** in Article 1:
> "本办法为XX证券旧版费用报销制度文本,仅作为制度沿革留档。"

This is a clean, unambiguous replacement: the 2025 version took effect January 1, 2025, and the 2022 version was repealed on the same date. No transition period is specified; the replacement is immediate upon the effective date.

## 3. Department General Manager (部门总经理) Approval Threshold

### 2022 Version (Deprecated)
- **Threshold**: ≥ 8,000 RMB (达到8000元)
- **Citation**: `02_expense_reimbursement_2022_deprecated.md`, Article 2 (第二条):
  > "旧版制度曾规定,单笔报销金额达到8000元的,应提交部门总经理审批。"
- **Status**: This threshold is **no longer valid** and "不得作为现行审批依据" (must not be used as current approval basis).

### 2025 Version (Current)
The 2025 version **does not** contain approval thresholds in its own text. Instead, Article 5 (第五条) delegates:
> "单笔费用报销的审批权限,不在本办法正文列示具体金额,统一按《XX证券授权管理办法》附件二执行。"

The current threshold comes from `03_authorization_management.md`, Attachment 2 (附件二):

| Tier | Amount Range | Required Approver |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| AR-01 | 0 ≤ amount < 3,000 | 部门经理 |
| AR-02 | 3,000 ≤ amount < 10,000 | 部门经理 + 财务复核 |
| AR-03 | **10,000 ≤ amount < 50,000** | **部门总经理** |
| AR-04 | 50,000 ≤ amount < 200,000 | 分管副总 |
| AR-05 | ≥ 200,000 | 总经理办公会 |

**Current department general manager threshold**: ≥ **10,000 RMB** (and < 50,000 RMB).

## 4. Which Version Currently Applies

**The 2025 revision (`01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md`) currently applies**, effective January 1, 2025.

The 2022 version is explicitly repealed and serves only as a historical/archival reference.

For the specific question of department general manager approval threshold, the applicable sources are:
1. `01_expense_reimbursement_2025.md`, Article 5 (第五条) — delegates authority
2. `03_authorization_management.md`, Attachment 2 (附件二), AR-03 — sets the actual threshold at ≥ 10,000 RMB

The business date is July 21, 2026, which is after the January 1, 2025 effective date, so the 2025 version applies without question.
